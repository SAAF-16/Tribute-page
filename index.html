History of Yeezy shoes

The brand who saved a billion styles

Here are some of the most popular releases

Through a combination of factors including popularization by celebrities, Adidas Yeezy sneakers have come to be extremely influential and highly coveted. Most of the shoes utilize Adidas's Boost material

  • December 3, 2013, Adidas confirmed a new shoe collaboration deal with West. The initial release of the Adidas Yeezy Boost 750 "Light Brown" was limited to 9000 pairs and sold out within 10 minutes.
  • June 27, 2015, the second shoe from the collaboration, the Yeezy Boost 350 was made available through a worldwide release.
  • October 29, 2015 the Yeezy 950 was released at select retail stores and online.
  • March 28, 2017 the Adidas Powerphase Calabasas was released
  • August 12, 2017, he Adidas Yeezy Boost 700 was released after the Yeezy Season 5
  • Desert Rat Colorway - This Adidas Yeezy 500 was released in December 2017
  • The first Adidas Yeezy Boost 380 was released in December 2019, as a surprise release on West's clothing launch site Yeezy Supply
  • The Yeezy Foam Runner is the first Adidas Yeezy shoe made in the United States, and first released exclusively through Yeezy Supply on June 26, 2020.
